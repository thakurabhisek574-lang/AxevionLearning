Q8. Derive the expression for Torque acting on a rectangular current loop in a uniform magnetic field.

Derivation Steps:
1. Consider a loop of length l and breadth b in field B.
2. Forces on the upper and lower arms act along the same line and cancel.
3. Force on the side arm: F = I l B.
4. Perpendicular distance between forces = b sinθ.
5. Torque τ = Force × Perpendicular distance.
6. τ = (I l B) × (b sinθ).
7. Area of loop A = l × b.
8. τ = I A B sinθ.
9. For N turns: τ = N I A B sinθ.
10. In vector form: τ = M × B (where M = NIA).
